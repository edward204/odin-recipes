Proper Banging Oreo Cheesecake

Description

This absolutely unreal Oreo cheesecake is yummy and easy to make and is perfect for sharing with family and friends. A crunchy Oreo base, smooth Oreo cheesecake filling and even more Oreos on top, it is the ultimate dessert for Oreo lovers!

Ingredients

For the base

  • 150g Oreos
  • 40g butter

For the cheesecake mixture

  • 235ml double cream
  • 235g cream cheese
  • 75g caster sugar
  • 115g Oreos

To decorate

  • 75ml double cream
  • 8 Oreos

Instructions

Make the biscuit base

  1. Line the base of an 18cm round tin with greaseproof paper.
  2. Melt the butter.
    Crush the Oreo biscuits.
    Mix together the crushed biscuits and melted butter. Put the biscuit mix into the tin and use the back of a spoon to push it flat.
    Put the tin into the fridge to chill while you make the filling.

Make the cheesecake filling

  1. Finely crush your Oreo biscuits
  2. Whip the double cream until it forms stiff peaks.
  3. Add the cream cheese and caster sugar and whisk again until the mixture forms stiff peaks and add the crushed Oreos and whisk onto the mixture.
  4. Remove the cheesecake base from the fridge. Add the cheesecake mixture to the tin. Use the back of a spoon to push the mixture down. Smooth the top with a palette knife or the back of a spoon.
  5. Return the cheesecake to the fridge for a minimum of 2 hours to chill.

Decorate the cheesecake

  1. Remove the cheesecake from the fridge and remove the tin. If the edges are smudged, use a palette knife around the edge to smooth it.
  2. Whip the double cream until it forms stiff speaks. Put it into a piping bag with a star nozzle and pipe 8 stars of cream around the edge of the cheesecake.
  3. Finally add an Oreo to each star of cream and serve.
